Philip Dean has been studying Shorin-Ryu Matsumura Seito karate with Sensei Wayne Dean since 2002. Philip also studied under the late Sensei Fred Sypher who was a guest instructor at Sensei Wayne Dean's Dojo in Hollywood Maryland. Philip serves as Sensei Dean's primary instructor for the dojo's [youth classes](/classes/).
