Whether you're a newcomer, experienced, or advanced, we now offer karate classes tailored to the experience and maturity level of all students.

| Class        | Requirements                                                                                               | Features                                                                                                                                                                     |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Youth        | Ages 4-8                                                                                                   | Basic self-defense tailored to children. Students will engage in enriching and fun activities designed to work out excess energy and foster discipline and respect.          |
| Intermediate | Students must meet a strict standard of proficiency, diligence, and maturity to be enrolled in this class. | Intermediate self-defense, forms, and traditional weapons training for the serious student. Students will engage in rigorous endurance activities and in partnered training. |
| Adult        | Ages 16 and up.                                                                                            | Comprehensive Shorin-Ryu training including empty-hand kata, bunkai, kobudo (weapons) training, and traditional Okinawan self-defense applications.                          |
