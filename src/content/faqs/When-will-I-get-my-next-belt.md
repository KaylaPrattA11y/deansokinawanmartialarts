---
question: When will I get my next belt?
sortPriority: 3
---

We are glad you are eager to progress, but please take note: _asking an instructor when you or your child will receive a new belt is considered disrespectful_ because it shifts the focus from learning and self-improvement to entitlement and rank chasing.

Our instructors are happy to provide feedback regarding areas of improvement and where more attention and focus is needed.
